What is WiseCook?

Are you tired of having the same meal pattern every day? With WiseCook, you'll never be running out of recipe ideas again!

As a home cook, it is your job to think of amazing meals every day. WiseCook will find the best matching recipes for you based on the ingredients you have in your pantry. Recipe suggestions can be filtered by cuisine or meal type.

Sometimes, we don't know what to do with our ingredients or leftovers from last night's dinner, we just throw them away. Using WiseCook, you can minimize food waste and save money at the same time. Whether you're a professional chef or just a home cook, WiseCook is a great tool.

Main Features:

  • Find recipes by ingredients
  • Dictate ingredients to add your pantry, no need to type if you don't want to
  • Filter your recipe suggestions based on cuisine or meal type
  • Mark ingredients if they have them or not
  • View suggested recipe based on your diets (e.g. vegan, vegetarian, keto)
  • Save your favorite recipes to view them quickly next time
  • Built-in shopping list for your ingredients

Note: Not all ingredients, meal types, and cuisines are supported, but there may be improvements in the future versions. Currently, the app only supports the following:

Meal types: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, Snacks, Side Dish, Main Dish, Dessert

Cuisines: Mediterranean, Italian, Indian, Filipino, European, Asian, American, Mexican, French, Thai, United States
